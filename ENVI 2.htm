June 2021 – July 2022
Off

About the campaign

CERN's Year of environmental awareness campaign was launched in June 2021 to inform and raise awareness of the Organization’s environmental challenges, its efforts in meeting them and how we can all contribute to reduce the Laboratory’s environmental footprint.

On this page, you will find all articles and infographics that have been published in the Bulletin over the course of the campaign, as well as the different event and activities that have been organised.
